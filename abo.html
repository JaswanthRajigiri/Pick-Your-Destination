Disrupting Travel Industry Since 2015...,Step by Step.

PickyourDestination, started in 2015, is India’s biggest online D-I-Y holiday booking platform that empowers users to curate customised holidays. The unique itinerary building platform considers factors like a traveller’s interests, trip duration, and budget to deliver the brand promise of ‘Craft Your Holiday, Your Way’, completely online. The travel company works in liaison with leading tourism boards of destinations like Australia, Singapore, Iceland, Japan, Dubai, New Zealand, besides 1,200+ hospitality partners around the world. PickyourDestination was one of the Official Sub-agents for the International Cricket Council (ICC) Women’s T20 World Cup.

PickyourDestination was born out of our desire to spread the magic of travel through the logic of tech. When you use PickyourDestination to create, customize and book trips, great care goes into replicating the adrenaline rush and sense of control of self-planning. At the same time, we also ensure that technology does all the heavy lifting - personalization, curation, booking, reminders, on trip recommendations, etc. All of this to give you that hassle-free trip that you worked so hard year long. Go ahead, unwrap the world.
